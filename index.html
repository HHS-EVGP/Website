What We Do

We are a group of students at Harrisonburg High School who design, build, and race an electric car to compete in the Electric Vehicle Grand Prix. As of 2026, we are a five-year-old team.

Sharing Our Knowledge

As student innovators, we believe that the lessons and experiences we learn should be shared, not guarded as secrets. For this reason, we publish our code and designs on GitHub and are working on setting up a YouTube channel with informational videos.

Results from the 2026 Washington, DC, and Shenandoah races

We finished 2nd in our class at the 2026 DC EVGP and 1st in our class at the 2026 Shenandoah Valley EVGP. Congratulations to all our hardworking team members!

Applause to the people behind Car 111, who went from a car that hadn't driven in years to a raceable car in just a few weeks!
